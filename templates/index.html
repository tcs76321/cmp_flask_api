Welcome to The Comprehensive Metabolic Panel API

Made by Trevor Stahl


This API allows you to quickly and efficiently determine if a specific metabolite is within healthy ranges

We use range references established by the AMERICAN BOARD OF INTERNAL MEDICINE


These are the metabolites we currently support, their healthy ranges, and the units we calculate in:

  • albumin | 35 to 55 g/L
  • blood urea nitrogen (bun) | 80 to 200 mg/L
  • calcium | 86 to 102 mg/L
  • carbon dioxide (co2) | 23 to 28 mEq/L
  • chloride | 98 to 106 mEq/L
  • creatinine | 5 to 13 mg/L
  • glucose | 700 to 990 mg/L
  • potassium | 35 to 50 mEq/decaL
  • sodium | 136 to 145 mEq/L
  • total bilirubin (bilirubin) | 3 to 10 mg/L
  • total protein (protein) | 55 to 90 g/L
  • alanine aminotransferase (alt) | 10 to 40 U/L
  • alkaline phosphatase (alp) | 30 to 120 U/L
  • aspartate aminotransferase (ast) | 10 to 40 U/L

* Use the string inside parentheses when they are present for multi-word metabolites
In example: blood urea nitrogen should be called with 'bun'
